What We Do

In the HCI lab at UCSC, we're working on exciting research in Human-Computer Interaction. Our team is involved in a Google-funded project where we're exploring the impact of AI and developing interventions to address its potential harms. By focusing on user needs, testing new ways to interact with technology, and ensuring our designs are practical, we aim to create technologies that improve everyday life.
